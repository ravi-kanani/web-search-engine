#92: Marlon Nichols – Deep Dive on Venture Capital with the Co-Founder of Cross Culture VC

Marlon Nichols is the Co-Founder & Managing Partner of Cross Culture Ventures, along with co-founder Troy Carter.

Marlon previously served as a Partner at Intel Capital. He has worked with several startups, invested in companies like Mayvenn, Gimlet Media, Blavity, Airspace Technologies, Codeverse, and many others.

Marlon is huge on CULTURE when it comes to uncovering hidden opportunities. Today, he talks about the power of culture and diversity, the value of mentorship, and how to assemble a winning team.

[01:40] Why the name Cross Culture Ventures? The major sectors they focus on as well as their stats and trends. Learn about what drives popular culture!

[06:05] Find out why Marlon decided to invest in Mayvenn, a platform for hairstylists in underserved communities, allowing them also sell hair, bringing more revenue in.

[11:30] Marlon shares some tips about building your team and where you can find your talent pool. He talks about how he found his amazing team.

[23:00] They're hiring an MBA intern! Find out if you're the one they're looking for.

[24:50] Why they invested in both Blavity and Gimlet Media, how they're both different, and why they invest in different locations around the U.S. and around the world

[33:15] Marlon talks about where the venture capital world and the tech world are going and how those revolve around culture.

[34:55] When asked if he only had $100 to move to a new city, Marlon emphasizes that it's less about money and more about strategic approach.

[37:00] The value of mentorship: Learn about what Marlon actually learned from his mentors when he was building his VC.

[40:00] Visit their report from State of Tech and Culture, Culture A$ Currency. Marlon expounds on the impact of culture in the success of your company.

[42:00] How to overcome impostor syndrome: Level up your weaknesses, but focus on your strengths
