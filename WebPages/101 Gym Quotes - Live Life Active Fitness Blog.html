101 Gym Quotes

Looking for some good workout or gym quotes?  You’ve come to the right place!  This list is everything from inspirational to funny quotes that will help inspire you to get to the gym and Live Life Active. Here is the list of my all time favorite gym quotes.  Leave a comment below if you have one that you’d like to add.

1. Pain is temporary, pride is forever.

2. I’m doing it for ME.

3. If you still look cute after the gym, you didn’t workout hard enough.

4. Sweat is your fat crying.

5. I wear black to the gym because it’s like a funeral for my fat.

6. Fitness is not about being better than someone.  Fitness is about being better than the person you were yesterday.

7. Be the girl at the gym that motivates everyone else to push a little harder.

8. Take care of your body. It’s the only place you have to live.

9. We all have the same amount of hours in our day.  Deciding how to use your hours is up to you.

10. Gotta squat before I tie the knot.
