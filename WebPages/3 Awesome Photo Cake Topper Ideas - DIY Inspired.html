Honor your Guests with Pictures with these Fun Photo Cake Topper Ideas

Images and photos are everything these days. People are constantly taking pictures on their phones of what they are eating, what they are wearing and who they are with.  They post their daily habits on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter all day long.

With access to so many photos, I think it’s fun to put them to good use other than leaving them in some virtual black hole…I mean cloud, ESPECIALLY when you are celebrating something. I put together a compilation of three fun and creative photo cake topper ideas. You can use these ideas for:

  • Birthdays
  • Anniversaries
  • Graduations
  • Showers
  • Father’s/Mother’s Day
  • Teacher Appreciation
  • Retirement Parties
  • Work Events
  • Holiday Parties
  • Anything!

Step By Step Instructions:

These 3D photo cupcake toppers are the best! They are so easy and honestly it doesn’t have to be a special occasion for you to make them. Print a bunch of pictures of your friends and make these for a casual get together. If you don’t have a circle punch, you can make these with squares or rectangles.

Make this fun photo cake banner. I made this particular banner for my husband on Father’s Day. I printed a bunch of photos from my phone of him, him and me together, and of course some of the girls and him.

Lastly, I made this photo collage topper for my brother on his birthday. The new baby in our family (ok, she’s almost 2 but…) was born with a birthday two days apart from my brother.  I didn’t want her birthday to overshadow his, so I made him this special cake topper with some fun photos of him that I found on Facebook.

Tip: You can grab photos from all your social media networks to find photos of your friends and family.

I hope you enjoyed these!
