Hello again, dear listeners, and welcome to another 5 questions episode.

In these episodes, if its not clear by the 36th of them, we take questions from Twitter (Thomas | Martin), Instagram (Thomas | Martin), email, and the College Info Geek Community on Reddit. We then answer them each in rapid-fire succession, speed-dating style.

Here are the questions weve chosen this time around:

  • What should you do to try to stay focused when youre at a job with an open door policy and interruptions are unavoidable?
  • Whats the best position to read in?
  • Do you hardcode all of the posts for CIG, or do you use a blogging platform?
  • How can I write an interesting social media bio?
  • Should I love a new hobby immediately, even if Im not good at it?
