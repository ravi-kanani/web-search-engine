Welcome to StateGiftsUSA.com

Jim and Laura, StateGiftsUSA

This site is owned and developed by Jim and Laura Hofman. We have always loved to travel and explore what makes American cities (and small towns) unique. We are big advocates of “buying local” and have discovered countless specialty products, edible and otherwise, across the United States.

StateGiftsUSA.com has been online since November, 2013. We are now the largest and most continuously updated “Made In America” website online, with new small businesses added weekly.

Our numerous travels over the years hatched the idea of developing this website as an informational resource for those seeking a unique specialty gift or simply “a taste of home”.

Please bookmark this site and check back often. We are continually adding to the selections for each state, in fact we add new products every week. Our immediate plans call for 50+ “Made In” products for each state and ultimately at least 100.

If you know of small local companies who sell interesting and unique products online, feel free to contact us and we very well could add them on the appropriate page!

This site is dedicated to my father, Robert C. Hofman, who spent a lot of time and money years ago taking our family to interesting places across the United States. His first question upon arriving in a new city or town was always, “What’s the local beer?” 

Thank you so much for visiting StateGiftsUSA.com.

Jim and Laura

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS …

How can I get my business listed on State Gifts USA? Visit our “Advertise With Us” page for all the details.

Is there a fee to be listed on State Gifts USA? We are an advertising supported site and offer a featured listing of your business / products for a ONE TIME fee of just $25. We write the feature for you, add images, link to your business website and sales page, along with a few more perks. Just tell us your website address and we’ll take it from there!

I’d like to buy some of the products listed on your site. Do you have a catalog? No, we are an informational site designed to publicize small U.S. businesses from all 50 states. We do not stock, sell, or ship any products. In each feature listed on our site, you’ll note we link to their website so you can buy directly from the source … and not a middle man. Our site provides advertising and publicity for small businesses across the U.S.

Do you accept product samples for review to potentially add to my state’s page> Occasionally, yes, we do. Please reach out to us at our Contact page to discuss.
