About me...
The Ed Tech Roundup was formed in early 2013 with the goal of helping educators with technology integration.  We provide a roundup of the latest educational technology news as well as a collection of reviews, lesson plans, suggested applications, and professional development ideas. Plus, our site has been selected as one of the Top 50 K-12 I.T. Blogs by Ed Tech Magazine and as a Top 50 Blog by Getting Smart.

The Roundup is run by Mike Karlin, PhD - an award-winning educator with over a decade of classroom experience.  Mike has taught at every level from preK-16 and received his PhD from Indiana University's Instructional Systems Technology program.  He is also the Director of Instructional Design for Tiber Health, as well as the lead author and instructor for ISTE's Computational Thinking course.
