    The Skinny on American Eagle

    American Eagle Outfitters may have built its reputation on the heritage of its denim, but it has spread its wings over the past 38 years to become a global name in stylish, casual clothing. From cozy parkas and chunky knitwear to floral jumpsuits and head-turning swimwear, young women can dress for all seasons with the American Eagle collection to hand. The menswear range is equally impressive with comfortable, collegiate hoodies and hip plaid shirts on offer alongside board shorts and flip-flops for the warmer summer months. Shop the American Eagle range and you’ll be flying high on all-American style without busting your budget.

    Does American Eagle offer free shipping?

    US customers are in luck! American Eagle offers free standard shipping (3-5 business days) on orders of $50 or more. Orders below this value will be charged a flat rate of $7 for standard shipping. However, if you can’t wait this long for your order, we don’t blame you. Please check the American Eagle shipping page for faster options.

    Does American Eagle accept returns?

    American Eagle is happy to accept returns for refunds or exchanges. You can return your order to a store for free or pay for the return postage and send by mail. Please see the American Eagle returns page for more detailed instructions.

    Can I buy an American Eagle gift card online?

    Absolutely! Give your loved ones the gift of fashion and purchase a gift card from the American Eagle website. You can choose whether you would prefer to mail a physical card or send an e-gift card by email or text.
