Engadget Review

Bang & Olufsen maintains its stellar audio clarity and provides good overall sound on its third version of the E8. Handy features like ambient sound, wireless charging and sound customization are also nice touches. However, for $350, active noise cancellation should be on the spec sheet, too.

The B&O Beoplay E8 3rd Gen are excellent true wireless earbuds and an ideal purchase for any avid music listener or fashion-conscious wearer with cash to burn. They offer one the most attractive designs on a true wireless set of earbuds, plus wonderfully balanced audio quality. The downside is they don’t feature ANC.
