The list of the best WordPress video themes to create the next Youtube, Vimeo or to showcase videos and movies in style.

Video has become an important part of a commodity in our society. Thanks to growing smartphone and tablet adoption, cheaper data tariffs and better cameras people have started to take a lot of videos. Some of the most popular videos on Youtube are shot using smartphones. It has become insanely difficult to stand out between other industry professionals due to increasing competition. Meanwhile, everyone can make the next big thing on Youtube and make millions of views.
