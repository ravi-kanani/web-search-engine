About BiggerPockets

We are a community of like-minded individuals, here to motivate and help you move toward financial success—whether that means landing your first investment property, expanding your current portfolio, networking with fellow investors and vendors, or simply bettering your financial situation. We host regularly updated resources ranging from free content in the form of forums, blog articles, podcasts, and videos to deal-evaluating calculators and a paid suite of tools and perks for Plus, Pro, and Premium members. BiggerPockets also features a Marketplace—where real estate investors can list and search for off-market, investor-centric real estate deals—and a bookstore with published materials on investment techniques and real estate strategies for beginners and experts alike.

BiggerPockets is a complete resource for anyone looking to succeed in real estate investing
