    When it comes to vaginas and vulvas, different is normal.

    But research shows 65% of 26-35 year olds have a problem using the terms vagina and vulva.

    That’s why we teamed up with Canesten to challenge stigma, normalise conversation and empower you to take charge of, and celebrate, your body and your health.
