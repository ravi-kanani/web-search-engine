About BoB


Welcome! I'm Manouk, a travel writer and adventurer from the Netherlands. Despite being a medical doctor, I still haven't found a cure for my travel bug! So, I keep on exploring. I love the great outdoors, learning about different cultures and backpacking in offbeat destinations. I'm also slightly obsessed with mountain lakes. Follow me for

  • expert travel advice
  • down to earth trip reports
  • extraordinary adventures
