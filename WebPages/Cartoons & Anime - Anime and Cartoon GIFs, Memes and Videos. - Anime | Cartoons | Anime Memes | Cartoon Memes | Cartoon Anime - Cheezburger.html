                        • Tweet
                        • Stumble
                        • Pin It
                        • Email

                        You don't have to be a fan of the manga series Naruto to appreciate these relatable object-labeling memes. This format is from the show's first episode, taken from a scene in which Naruto hides from Mizuki behind a tree. The meme is pretty simple in that people are using it to depict any situation involving hiding. The possibilities for this format are endless, which is probably why it's stuck around since early summer!
