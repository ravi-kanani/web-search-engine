Category Accessibility

Websites aren’t just meant to look good — they are meant to be easy to use for everybody: people who rely on speech, are color-blind or live with dementia. There is no such thing as a normal user, or average user: keeping our content accessible to whatever comes into user’s way is critical for keeping content future-ready.
