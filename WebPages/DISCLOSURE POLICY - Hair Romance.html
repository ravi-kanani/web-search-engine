DISCLOSURE POLICY

Hair Romance is a personal hair blog written and edited by Christina Butcher. I do occasionally accept forms of cash advertising, sponsorship, paid insertions or other forms of compensation but will always make you aware of when this is the case. I only want to share things worth your time.

Hair Romance abides by word of mouth marketing standards. Hair Romance believes in honesty of relationship, opinion and identity. The compensation received may influence the advertising content, topics or posts made in this blog. That content, advertising space or post will be clearly identified as paid or sponsored content.

As owner of Hair Romance, I am occasionally compensated to provide opinion on products, services, websites and various other topics. Even though Hair Romance sometimes receives compensation for our posts or advertisements, I, and any contributors, always give our honest opinions, findings, beliefs, or experiences on those topics or products. The views and opinions expressed on Hair Romance are purely the the writers’ own. Any product claim, statistic, quote or other representation about a product or service should be verified with the manufacturer, provider or party in question.

This blog does not contain any content which might present a conflict of interest.

To get your own policy, go to http://www.disclosurepolicy.org

What does this mean?

By having a disclosure policy, it means you can trust that when there’s no mention, there’s no sponsored content involved.

Hair Romance is fortunate to receive product from PR companies, and works with brands on producing sponsored content, but always with you, the reader, in mind. You’ll always read honest opinions on Hair Romance.

I appreciate you taking time to read Hair Romance, and I want you to love your hair. You can find tips, tricks and styling advice here knowing that I only recommend products and services that I believe in.
