Darren Rowse: Speaker

Note: To book Darren to speak at your event please contact his agent Laney Galligan via email at Agents of Influence.

Why Book Darren Rowse to Speak at Your Next Event?

Darren Rowse is the founder of ProBlogger, Digital Photography School and numerous other online businesses.

World Domination Summit 2013: Photo by Joshua Seaman

He is a sought-after keynote speaker who has spoken around the world at events like South by South West (Austin, Texas), World Domination Summit (Portland Oregon), Authority Intensive/CopyBlogger Event (Denver, Colorado), Mumbrella 360 (Sydney Australia), Tropical Think Tank (Philippines), New Media Expo (Las Vegas and Los Angeles), Social Media Week (Sydney Australia), Social Media Expo (San Diego), Inbound (Boston) and many more.

Darren speaks on a range of topics including topics relevant to helping bloggers improve their blogs, helping brands to understand the blogging and social media landscape, on topics of entrepreneurship, self improvement, philanthropy and much more.

He’s delivered keynotes to audiences of thousands and workshops to small groups.

Darren also runs and speaks at Australia’s leading blogging conference – the ProBlogger Training Event – an annual event for 550+ Australian bloggers.

Darren lives in Melbourne Australia but regularly travels to speak around Australia and internationally.

For more details please contact Darren’s agent Laney Galligan via email at Agents of Influence.

Video Examples of Darren Speaking

Inbound: Boston 2015

Darren was invited to give a ‘Bold Talk’ at Hubspot’s Inbound conference in 2015. The challenge was to give a 12 minute talk to 1000 entrepreneurs and marketers but to speak about something other than his normal topic (that of blogging). Darren chose to speak about Serendipity and Luck in Entrepreneurship.

In this video Darren speaks to 3500 people in the main theatre of the world renowned World Domination Summit. Darren shares his story and gives tips on ‘making dreams come true’.
