    The Skinny on DICK's Sporting Goods

    DICK’S Sporting Goods started in 1948 when an 18-year-old Dick Stack quit his job at an Army Surplus Store to start up his own business selling fishing supplies. Although Dick’s grandma loaned him just $300 to start his business, it grew and grew to become America’s favourite supplier of sport products. From the East Coast to the West, DICK’s Sporting Goods is a name sports fans know they can rely on. Stocking everything from hockey sticks to yoga matts and from kayaks to bicycles, there isn’t a sport on Earth DICK’s doesn’t cater to. We caught up with Dick to talk shop.

    How can you find your nearest DICK’s Sporting Goods?

    Wherever you live, you can’t be too far from a DICK’S Sporting Goods. They’ve got stores everywhere from Tallahassee to Tucson and everywhere from Alabama to Alaska. Use the store finder here to find your nearest DICK’s.

    If you’re not totally happy with your order, what is the DICK’S Sporting Goods returns policy?

    DICK’s is a good sport, and if you’re not entirely happy with your purchase for whatever reason, will offer a 60-day return policy on most products. It’s worth noting that, if you’re returning an unwanted present, items returned with a gift receipt will be offered a gift card or an even exchange.

    What are the different shipping options from DICK’s Sporting Goods?

    DICK’s Sporting Goods offers a wide range of shipping options, and the best choice for you will probably vary depending on the size of your item, and how quickly you need to get your hands on it. You can read more about the different shipping options here.

    How do you use a DICK’s Sporting Goods coupon?

    Once you’ve finished shopping and found everything you’re after, add the products to your shopping cart and click through to get to checkout. Once you’ve signed in or entered your customer information, simply paste your code in when prompted.

    What is a DICK’s Sporting Goods Scorecard?

    Sign up to DICK’s Sporting Goods Scorecard membership scheme and you’ll receive one point for every dollar you spend at DICK'S Sporting Goods,then a $10 Reward for every 300 points you collect. You’ll also be sent regular Bonus Point coupons via mail and email that you can redeem for huge savings on all of DICK’s amazing range.
