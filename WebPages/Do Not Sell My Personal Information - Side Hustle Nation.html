Do Not Sell My Personal Information

CONSUMER’S RIGHT TO REQUEST BUSINESS NOT SELL INFORMATION

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, consumers have the right to request that a business that collected personal information not sell that information to anyone.

As stated in our CCPA policy, Side Hustle Nation does not sell your personal information. Still, if you’re a current or former email subscriber of Side Hustle Nation, and want to be extra sure in the event of a possible future sale, you can email privacy@sidehustlenation.com.

In your email, use the subject line “CCPA opt-out” and the body “Please don’t sell my personal information.” Make sure to email from the same email address that’s in our system. A tag will be added to your customer record indicating your request.
