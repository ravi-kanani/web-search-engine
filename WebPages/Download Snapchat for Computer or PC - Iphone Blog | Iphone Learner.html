    Download Snapchat for Computer or PC

    With tons and tons of instant messaging app, its very difficult to choose the one which will be the best of all. But only very few apps from these tons of app could make into the top of the lis. Some of such apps could be whatsapp, facebook messenger, wechat, hike and snapchat.

    Snapchat is surely one of the fastest growing instant messaging app after its introduction in the year 2011. It has gained its popularity very fast and you can hear every next person is talking about this fantastic app for instant messaging and snap sharing .

    About SnapChat

    Today a large number of people are using instant messaging to communicate with each other andout of this a large number of people is using snapchat to communicate . What makes this app so popular among the youth? The reason for for its growing popularity amongst youth is its unique feature that after 24 hours the snaps shared using this app , i.e., videos , text or photo will be deleted automatically so as to make room for the new chats.

    Using this unique feature of automatic deletion of your picture or snaps , you can share your   pictures with your friends and family like selfies or any other unique picture that you want your friends and family to have a look on it.

    Snapchat for PC Free Download 

    Snapchat is available on multiple platforms like android and iOS . You can download this app from Google play store or from Apple app store. But Snapchat is not available for your PC or laptops.  But no need to worry because we brings for you the following steps by which you can download and use Snapchat on your Pc or computer for free.

    1. The first step is to download  iPadian , which is an android emulator, for your pc or computer. It is free .
    2. Install iPadian into your pc or computer.
    3. Type “Snapchat ” in the search box which is at the top right corner of iPadian.
    4. Click on install option from the given results to download it on your PC or computer.
    5. Wait until the installation completes.
    6. Now to play the game click on the Snapchat from “My Apps” in iPadian.

    That’s all on how to download Snapchat for your pc for free. A tip for using iPadian, stop the application when it is not in use it will make your PC little slower.
