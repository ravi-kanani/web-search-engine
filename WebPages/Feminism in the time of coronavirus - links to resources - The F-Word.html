Feminism in the time of coronavirus – links to resources

Various Authors24 March 2020
Life0 Comments 0

Potentially useful links relating to Coronavirus (COVID-19) gathered by The F-Word team

We hope all F-Word readers are bearing up in these unprecedented times. We thought we’d gather a few links to resources / ideas / articles in one place in case it was useful. We’ll keep adding to this we find things. Stay safe out there.

Practical

https://www.understandinguniversalcredit.gov.uk/coronavirus/ – advice on claiming benefits
https://www.nmdunited.org/covid-19-guide – US-based plan and preparation guide for adults living with neuromuscular disabilities
https://www.citizensadvice.org.uk/health/coronavirus-what-it-means-for-you/ – useful information from Citizens Advice
https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/covid-19-guidance-on-social-distancing-and-for-vulnerable-people/guidance-on-social-distancing-for-everyone-in-the-uk-and-protecting-older-people-and-vulnerable-adults? – government guidance on social distancing
https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/covid-19-stay-at-home-guidance – Stay at home guidance for households with possible coronavirus (COVID-19) infection
https://fullfact.org/health/wuhan-coronavirus/ – Fact-checking site where you can make sure the information you’re reading online is accurate
https://twitter.com/MamaMeetsPod/status/1239364749122514944 – helpful hygiene tips from daughter of a microbiologist
https://twitter.com/WHO/status/1241134713575800834 – WHO Health Alert brings COVID-19 facts to billions via WhatsApp
https://action.asthma.org.uk/page/58019/donate/1 – Asthma UK Coronavirus (COVID-19): your guide to social shielding (also has link to donate)

Mutual aid

https://covidmutualaid.org/ – mutual aid groups
https://freedomnews.org.uk/covid-19-uk-mutual-aid-groups-a-list/ – more (the same?) mutual aid groups
https://www.theguardian.com/society/2020/mar/16/community-aid-groups-set-up-across-uk-amid-coronavirus-crisis – article on community / mutual aid groups

Mental health

https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/health-51873799 – managing mental health from BBC News
https://www.inverse.com/mind-body/7-science-based-strategies-to-cope-with-coronavirus-anxiety – strategies for anxiety
https://www.mind.org.uk/ – Mind, the mental health charity

Petitions and fundraising

https://www.swarmcollective.org/donate – sex worker hardship fund
https://www.crisis.org.uk/get-involved/in-this-together – fundraising for help for homeless people
https://www.anothermag.com/fashion-beauty/12362/help-indya-moore-raise-money-for-trans-people-of-colour-facing-coronavirus – help model and actor Indya Moore raise money for trans people of colour facing coronavirus
https://www.beonhand.co.uk/ – get help from, refer someone to or volunteer for On Hand (help for older people)
https://you.38degrees.org.uk/petitions/a-basic-income-for-all-citizens-during-the-coronavirus-crisis-months – basic income petition

Learning

https://www.jstor.org/open/ – open access to JSTOR
https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/here-are-380-ivy-league-courses-you-can-take-online-right-now-for-free-9b3ffcbd7b8c/ – 450 free online Ivy League courses
https://www.cambridge.org/about-us/covid-19/ – free textbooks and other resources from Cambridge University Press
https://about.muse.jhu.edu/resources/freeresourcescovid19/ – free access to Project Muse partner journals (most until 30 June 2020)
https://twitter.com/lvkerlvker/status/1240968584572125184 – 6-week online poetry course starting 30 March run by Ed Luker (£30-50)
https://learn.brook.org.uk/ – free e-learning resources

Entertainment

https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-features/livestreaming-concerts-coronavirus-967802/ – livestreamed concerts
https://www.travelandleisure.com/attractions/museums-galleries/museums-with-virtual-tours – museums with virtual tours
https://www.metopera.org/about/press-releases/met-to-launch-nightly-met-opera-streams-a-free-series-of-encore-live-in-hd-presentations-streamed-on-the-company-website-during-the-coronavirus-closure/ – opera streams from the Met
https://www.socialdistancingfestival.com/ – virtual festival
Renting films from Curzon Home Cinema (£7.99 or £9.99, or less if you have a membership)
Mubi is doing a three month trial for £1, giving access to lots of free old, foreign and independent films, and others you can rent for a few quid (one reader suggests you can add a note when you sign up that they should stop screening films by Roman Polanski)
BFI Flare, the LGBTIQ+ film festival has moved to BFI Player (ends on 29 March we think)
https://www.haymarketbooks.org/blogs/112-ten-free-ebooks-from-haymarket-books and
https://www.versobooks.com/blogs/4604-free-quarantine-ebooks (ends 2 April) – free eBooks
https://blog.scribd.com/home/2020/3/17/a-letter-from-the-scribd-ceo-to-our-community – ree access to Scribd’s digital library (ends 16 April)
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1kwDGZFH2-jFr4xZ2ynxQkihYwIVh_uZlp1-Q73uXzso/edit – a list of radical books and articles on black radicalism, racial capitalism, imperialism, gender and sexuality studies from @queersocialism
https://isolationfest.org/2020/03/20/about-us/ – Isolation Literary Festival, 16-19 April 2020 (submissions to become a performer are open until midnight on 6 April)
https://www.tiltedaxispress.com/blog/online-book-club – translated fiction online book club from Tilted Axis Press (starting 26 March 2020)

For children

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Z5H2sbcKd-j076UrILGU-hYLCJys3JOOM-ydyhva4Ts/edit – things to do with children at home, compiled by Gemma Murray
http://mimbre.co.uk/physical-and-creative-kids-activities-covid-19/ – physical and creative kids’ activities
