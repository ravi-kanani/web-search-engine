Fitness & Lifestyle Bloggers Wanted!

Atemi Sports Published: March 25, 2020
Location
Anywhere
Work can be done remotely
Job Type
Freelance
Category
Blog / Article Writing
Company Type
Small Business

Description

We're an e-commerce company who sell fitness products.

Currently looking to hire freelance writers to contribute to our website's blog - 1 to 3 articles per week, 1000-2000 words each.

We're looking for bloggers who have experience in writing about fitness and healthy lifestyle. You need to be knowledgeable in this area and able to write informative and readable pieces, for example reviews, workouts, diet plans, articles, lists etc. NO FLUFF!

The blog posts must be able to include several affiliate links to products on Amazon. Especially for home workout products like resistance bands, foam rollers, exercise balls etc, or things like supplements, fitness gadgets, and any other healthy lifestyle products.

You must be an experienced blogger and a native English speaker.

Ideally, you should be experienced in keyword research and SEO optimization, understanding how to create posts which rank well on Google. However, this is not essential as we can optimize the posts ourselves.

If you want to apply, please send:

-Your expected rate (per word)
-A brief intro
-At least 3 links to blog posts you have written before and which are currently online
