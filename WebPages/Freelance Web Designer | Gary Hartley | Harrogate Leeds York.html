About me

The Floating Frog is the home of me, Gary Hartley, an experienced freelance web designer from Harrogate, a beautiful spa town in North Yorkshire, UK. Traditionally trained in the fine art of graphic design and 10 successful years in two award-winning digital agencies behind me, I’m now enjoying the challenge and freedom of being a full time freelance web designer. I consider individual projects and in-house contract roles. I work with startups, internet marketers, business leaders, trusted brands and blue chip companies throughout the world.
