How It Works

With the HubSpot Blog Slack App, you can get the latest and greatest content from our Marketing and Sales blogs delivered directly via Slack -- only when you want it. The search feature will help you discover new resources on-demand for whatever topic you're interested in. 

 

Discover New Resources On-Demand in Slack

/hubspotblogs marketing [search term]
Get the top five articles on a particular topic from our Marketing Blog.

/hubspotblogs sales [search term]
Get the top five articles on a particular topic from our Sales Blog.
