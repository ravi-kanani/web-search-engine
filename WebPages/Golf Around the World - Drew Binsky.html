Golf Around the World

I am a 5-handicap golfer and I’ve played more than 300 rounds in 19+ countries.  Golf is my life. It’s my therapy.  Nothing makes me happier than being on a golf course.

Growing up in Scottsdale, Arizona, where there are more than 200 courses within a 50 mile radius of my house, golf was literally everywhere.  I couldn’t get enough.

When I was 14, I decided to take my game to the next level, so I signed up for weekly lessons from a former PGA pro named Scott Watkins.  Within one year, my handicap dropped from a 25 to a 7.  At my best, I was a 2 handicap (when I was 19), and now I play at about 5.  My lowest round ever was 70 (-2) at Kierland Country Club in Scottsdale, Arizona.

I keep every scorecard from all 300+ rounds of I’ve played, and even since I’ve been traveling the world full-time (without my clubs), I always jump on any opportunity I get to play a round.
