    So how do you find Groupon deals in your local area?

    It’s easy. Simply enter your Zip Code or neighborhood into the search bar at the top of the Groupon website and hit the search button.

    How do you use a Groupon promo code?

    It’s never been easier to snap up a great bargain. Once you’ve added your favorite Groupon deal to the cart, click on the ‘Checkout’ button. After you’ve signed in or made an account, locate the ‘Promotional Code’ box and enter your offer code into the box. Hit the ‘Apply’ button to redeem and your discount will be applied!

    If you place an order with Groupon marketplace, how much will it cost and how long will it take to arrive?

    Standard shipping is $3.99 on orders that are $34.99 and under. Orders over $34.99 qualify for free shipping. Groupon also offers speedy 3-day shipping in the US on select Groupon purchases - you will be notified of this at checkout.

    You can check the shipping rate on individual items at any time by entering your zip code into the box (beneath the Buy button) on the deal page.

    If you’re not happy with your Groupon marketplace purchase, can you return it for a refund?

    Of course. You can return most goods within 30 days for a refund or exchange (see the exchange FAQ). Items that are listed as ‘Final Sale’ cannot be returned unless defective. Refunds will be processed within 1-2 days. For help and more information, see the troubleshooting marketplace orders page.

    What if you’re not satisfied with your Groupon voucher/experience, can you claim a refund?

    Yes. If you’ve not enjoyed your experience, please contact Groupon within 14 days of your voucher use, trip or event. Any unredeemed local voucher can be returned for a refund within 3 days of purchase. If you purchase a getaways voucher and the merchant is unable to book your stay before the expiration date, Groupon will refund your purchase. If you haven’t booked your trip by the book-by date, the promotional value is no longer valid, but your voucher will still have paid credit. For more information, see The Groupon Promise policy.
