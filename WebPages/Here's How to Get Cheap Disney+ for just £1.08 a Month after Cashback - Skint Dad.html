Disney+ is launching on 24 March – but don’t just sign up! You can get cheap Disney+ for just £1.08 per month using this Quidco cashback deal.

Disney+ is launching in the UK on 24 March 2020.

Usually costing £59.99 if you sign up for a year there’s a way to get it much cheaper!

To celebrate the launch, they are running a pre-order deal so you only pay £49.99 for a year.

But, don’t just pay that because you can save even more!

Cheap Disney+ deal

Unfortunately, they have reached the limit on this offer. 

This offer has now expired.

They are hoping to launch a new offer next week, so watch this space.
 

Sign up for Quidco here and you can get £17 cashback when you sign up for Disney+ for a year.

That reduces the cost down to £32.99.

But, Quidco New Premium members will also receive £20 cashback on top of this.

With the £17 new member bonus, this will take the total cost to just £12.99! That’s £1.08 per month! 

How to get cheap Disney+

Here are the simple steps you need to get Disney+ mega cheap for a year.

  1. Sign-up to Quidco here
  2. Visit the Disney+ retailer page and click ‘Redeem Bonus’
  3. Shop as usual with Disney+ online and pay £49.99 
  4. You’ll then earn up to £20 cashback on your order – just for being a Quidco member
  5. Plus, you’ll receive a £17 cashback bonus from Quidco

That’s it!

So, you need to pay for the discounted year’s membership upfront but you will receive cashback in your Quidco account to cash out into your bank.

There are 2,500 of these offers up for grabs, and it’s on a first come first serve basis.

The offer is only running until 23 March and is for new members.

What is Quidco Premium?

Quidco have two levels of membership.

You can use the site and earn cashback on all your shopping absolutely free.

But, you can choose to upgrade to Quidco Premium which gives you extra features and chances to earn more.

Instead of getting basic cashback, you can earn up to 10% cashback rate top-ups!

You don’t need to pay from your own pocket to upgrade, but instead, give £1 of your monthly cashback earnings you get through Quidco.

If you don’t use Quidco for any reason for a month, your account will be considered inactive, you will continue to be a Premium member, and your account will be free from monthly fees.

And, when you can get mega cheap Disney+ using Quidco Premium is even better!
