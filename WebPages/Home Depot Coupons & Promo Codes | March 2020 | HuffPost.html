    The Skinny on Home Depot

    If you’re searching for hundreds of hardware and homeware products in one place, look no further than Home Depot. With more than 2,000 stores across the US, Canada, and Mexico, it’s no wonder Home Depot has become such a household name - that, and of course, its fantastic quality and low-cost prices.

    Whether you’re hoping to improve your interiors with a lick of paint or you think some added furniture will make your home more fabulous, you can trust in the support of Home Depot to turn your pad into a palace.

    I want to make an online. How do I use my Home Depot coupons?

    Select the coupons you wish to use and copy the code to your clipboard. Next, head over to the Home Depot website and start adding your items to the shopping cart. When you’re finished, click on the cart icon in the top right corner of the webpage. Here, locate the field marked ‘Have a promo code?’, paste your code and hit ‘Apply’.

    Does Home Depot offer free shipping?

    Absolutely. You’ll receive free shipping from Home Depot when your order amounts to more than $45.00. You can also opt to pick up your items for free from within your closest store.

    Other shipping options include 2-business days or next-day delivery for a small charge. To see exclusions on free delivery, check out the Shipping Information.

    I’m not happy with my purchases. Am I able to return my items to Home Depot?

    Yes. Either take your products back to your nearest Home Depot for an instant refund or exchange. You can also return items online by mail via the Returns Page. All items must be returned within 90 days of delivery and must be accompanied by a valid ID and/or the credit card used to pay for the items.

    Can I buy a Home Depot gift card?

    Yes, you can. A gift card is a great idea for any friend that loves DIY or has plans to renovate their home. Head to the gift card page and select whether you’d like to send one via mail or email. The minimum top-up is $25. You’ll also have the option to add a personal message so that your friend knows who has sent them such a fantastic gift.

    Where is my nearest Home Depot?

    Discover your nearest store by using the Store Finder - just type in your zip code, city or state into the designated box and hit the search button.
