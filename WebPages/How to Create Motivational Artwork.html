How to Create Motivational Artwork

What’s the best type of artwork? Artwork which motivates others, and yourself!

1. Artwork of those who you love

For me, artwork of Cindy. You cannot fake love.

If you make art works of your soulmate, there’s something inherently motivational in it. You were moved by that person, and that person has a huge impact in your life. Thus, your art works of them will have the strongest impact and soul.

2. Imputing your soul into your art works

Why I love calligraphy — I can impute my soul into my finger, into my calligraphs. Calligraphy is probably one of the most direct ways you can impute your life force into your artwork:

3. Make art works which entertain yourself!

You cannot fake enthusiasm. Only make art works of things you’re genuinely interested and curious in, and your enthusiasm and motivation will inspire, affect, and motivate others!

4. Stay curious

My personal curiosity in almost everything motivates me. So — obey your childlike curiosity and naïveté!

Create on!
