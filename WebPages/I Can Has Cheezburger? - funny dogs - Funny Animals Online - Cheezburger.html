Dog Was So Happy His Owner is Home Now That He Sprained His Tail From Too Much Wagging

  • Tweet
  • Stumble
  • Pin It
  • Email

Rolo is a 7-year-old dachshund from the United Kingdom.

When this good boy realized his owner, Emma Smith, is staying home due to the coronavirus pandemic, he couldn't contain his excitement and started wagging his tail so much that he, unfortunately, sprained it.  

His owner twitted the story which was shared by more than 140,000 times and liked by more than 1 million people, including "The Morning Show" and "Friends" star Jennifer Aniston.

Smith continued on updating about her happy pooch in the following days and even set up Rolo with his own Instagram account — rolo.thehotdog — so that interested pup lovers can "follow my journey getting my wag back."
