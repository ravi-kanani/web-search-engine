Camps Bay

Featured Area

Camps Bay

Crowds flock to this popular area which is well-known for its long stretch of white sandy beach, restaurants with tables spilling on to the sidewalk, and holiday atmosphere all year round.

Fresnaye

Featured Area

Fresnaye

Situated towards the west of Cape Town’s city centre, Fresnaye lies on the famed Atlantic Seaboard and boasts scenic walks and bewitching views of the Atlantic Ocean below.
