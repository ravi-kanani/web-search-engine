    Check out this series of amazing illustrations of famous actors created by Ricardo Polo.

    Ricardo Polo is a Madrid-based graphic designer and illustrator. He loves to play with vector graphics to create images for posters, books, magazines, T-shirts, logos, etc. In addition, he’s working on layouts for publications, posters, flyers, stationery, business cards, and many more design projects. The following series of portraits of famous actors proves his creativity and outstanding skills as an illustrator. For those of you who want to see more of Ricardo Polo’s creative work, please visit his website or follow him on Behance and Instagram. Some of his artworks are available for purchase on Society6.

    All images © by Ricardo Polo. If you are looking for more creative inspiration, you should probably check out our popular Illustration category. The section includes a curated collection of both handmade drawings and computer graphics created by some of the most talented illustrators from all over the world.
