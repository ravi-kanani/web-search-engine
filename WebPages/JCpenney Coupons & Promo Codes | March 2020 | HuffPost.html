    The Skinny on JCPenney

    Combining quality and convenience, JCPenney is every family’s one stop shop for stylish clothing, homewares and essentials. Treat your bedroom to some luxurious linens, freshen up your wardrobe with a statement shirt or stock your beauty shelves to the brim with the high quality, low cost range at JCPenney. Whether you’re looking to stock up on the essentials or splash out on a few new season pieces, we’ve asked all the questions you need to get the most out of your next haul.

    Can you buy a JCPenney gift card online?

    Yes, you can - choose from a physical gift card to be mailed to that special someone, or an e-gift card to be printed or sent over email if you’re in a hurry. You can choose the value of your gift and a fun or fashionable design to personalize the card. If you’re opting for an e-gift, you can even add your own photo or video to the card for a special touch!

    How do you redeem your JCPenney coupons?

    Choose the code you want to use and keep it handy. Head to the JCPenney website and shop the range as usual. When you’re ready to head to the checkout, select the cart icon at the top of the screen. Review your items and enter your coupon in the box provided above the subtotal to redeem.

    What is the JCPenney Salon?

    Save time and money by booking yourself into the JCP salon for haircuts, color, treatment and styling. Find a salon near you to make an appointment, and browse the top quality range of styling tools and hair care products, too.

    How much does JCPenney shipping cost?

    Shipping charges will vary depending on the overall value of your order. Standard shipping takes 3-5 working days and is free for orders over $99, or costs $8.95 for all other orders. Premium shipping takes three working days and costs $4.95 for orders over $99, or $9.95 for orders of less than this value.

    What’s the JCPenney returns policy if you’ve changed your mind?

    If you’re not happy with your order, don’t worry - you can return the item or exchange it, as long as you’ve got proof of purchase. Either return in-store or complete the ‘returns or exchange’ form that came with your invoice. If you’re opting for a refund, you’ll be reimbursed using the original method of payment.
