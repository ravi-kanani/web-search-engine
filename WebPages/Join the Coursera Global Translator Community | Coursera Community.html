What is the Global Translator Community?


The Global Translator Community (GTC) is a group of awesome Coursera learners who translate course video subtitles from English into more than 40 languages in their spare time. The GTC helps learners around the world transform their lives by making Coursera courses accessible to people who don't speak English. Anyone who is fluent in English and at least 1 other language is welcome to join!

What do members do?


  • Provide high-quality subtitle translations for Coursera course videos
  • Review existing translations to ensure quality and accuracy

Do members need to commit to anything?


No! Members can translate as much or as little as they want, whenever they want.
