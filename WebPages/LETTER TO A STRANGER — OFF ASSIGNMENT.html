LETTER TO A STRANGER

In 2013, Off Assignment began asking writers a simple question: "Who haunts you?" They responded in droves. They wrote letters.

THE LATEST LETTERS
Sarah Rovang
TO THE MAN NAMED "PRAISE GOD"
Sarah Rovang

“Against the noise, dust, and midday sun, I struggled to frame a shot of Kruger that looked much like anything. With elbows pinned to my sides, back slumped, I felt self-conscious and a little abashed. Why was I taking this picture? Was it historical documentation or vapid cultural voyeurism?”

Sarah Rovang

Fez
Lavinia Spalding

New York City, New York
Sarah Perry

Dakar, Senegal

TO THE ROOMFUL OF TRAVELERS WATCHING AMERICAN REALITY TV
La Paz, Bolivia
Anna Vodicka

They say that after a rain, a glassy surface of water settles over Bolivia’s salt flats, converting the land into the world’s largest mirror. I imagine the brujas, with their black braids and bowler hats, have something to do with this, holding a looking glass to the world. It takes a courage I didn’t have to gaze into it.

La Paz, Bolivia
Anna Vodicka
