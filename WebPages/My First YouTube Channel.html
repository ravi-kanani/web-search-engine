My First YouTube Channel

Living Life with Lex Uncategorized October 14, 2017November 27, 2017 1 Minute

I always dreamed of creating a YouTube channel. My friends say that I have a great personality, but I’m too lazy to edit videos. Editing videos takes hours to do. I would rather be sleeping than to stare at a computer screen all day long.

But this semester the tables were turned. I now had no choice but to create a YouTube channel. You probably want to ask why. Well creating a YouTube channel was a requirement for one of my classes. I bit the bullet and came to the realization that I can no longer be a lazy bum.

I decided that I wanted to do a lifestyle channel that was all about me. Here is a website you can look at that describes the different types of YouTube channels Blogging Types. If you are eager just as I was about starting a channel, look at this website to start your channel How to start a successful YouTube Channel for your business.

It was finally time to record my first video. I set the camera up in my living room and positioned myself in the center of the camera. I pressed the start button and I starred in the camera like a deer in headlights. Have you ever had so much to say but can’t put it in words? Well that was me. For a girl who talks so much on a daily basis, I instantly became shy as soon as the camera started rolling.

I eventually got my thoughts together and created an amazing introduction video. I was so proud of the finish product and I hope that you like it too.

If you like my video, go like, and subscribe.
