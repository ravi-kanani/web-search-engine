New York Launch Pod Wins 2016 New York Press Club Award For Best Podcast!

May 18, 2016 By Hal Coopersmith in News, Post Tags: Award for Best Podcast, New York Press Club

We’re pleased to let you know that the New York Launch Pod won the New York Press Club award for best podcast in 2016!  The New York Launch Pod joins the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, WNYC, and NPR in receiving awards from the New York Press Club this year.  This is the first time the New York Press Club has ever given the award for best podcast and an incredible honor since many of the other recipients also have stand alone podcasts.  For the official New York Press Club press release click here: https://www.nypressclub.org/docs/j-awards/2016-jawards-winners-news-release.pdf
