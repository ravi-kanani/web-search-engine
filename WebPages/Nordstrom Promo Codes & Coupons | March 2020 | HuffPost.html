    The Skinny on Nordstrom

    Nordstrom has come a long way since starting out as a little store in Seattle. Beginning operations in 1901, Nordstrom have always worked to deliver the best possible service and exceed expectations with their range of products.

    Nordstrom dresses, including the latest Burberry and Fendi, will set your next evening alight. Nordstrom’s selection of men’s boots, including pieces by Gucci and its peers, are the seasonal masterpieces you need. In fact, no matter where you shop in Nordstrom, you’ll be bound to find your new favorite things.

    When do I use my Nordstrom promo code?

    First, you’ll have to fill your shopping bag up with all the fashion goodies that your heart desires.

    Then, once you begin to checkout, you’ll have to choose between signing in, or continuing as guest. We highly recommend the former option.

    The second stage is choosing your shipping method, which depends on how urgently you need your new favorite things.

    The third stage is payment, which is where you’ll enter your code. Just above the ‘Review Order’ button, you’ll see a link that says “Apply Promo Code”. Click on that to reveal a text box where you’ll need to type your code. Just don’t forget to hit 'Apply'!

    You mentioned something about shipping. What are my options with Nordstrom?

    If you’re happy to wait around three to six business days, standard shipping is your best bet. It’s totally free, and even Alaska and Hawaii are included.

    If you need your things a little quicker, or only available for deliveries on a Saturday, you must order before 3.00pm and pay the additional charge for expedited shipping. Lucky shoppers in a selection of zip codes may even qualify for Same Day delivery.

    And there is always the option of having your order sent to your nearest Nordstrom store for pickup.

    What is Nordstrom like with their return policy?

    Put simply, Nordstrom handles all their returns on a case-by-case basis. At the end of the day, their aim is to make you happy, which is something they’ve been doing for customers for a long time, so get in contact with them and they will be happy to accommodate.

    Is there a Nordstrom nearby to me?

    Nordstrom has plenty of locations, but the quickest way to find your nearest one is via the website. There is a store finder if you scroll down to the footer and click ‘Stores & Events’ under ‘About Us’. Alternatively, click here.

    Do Nordstrom allow product reviews on their website?

    They certainly do! Any product that has been rated, which includes more than just a few across the range, will have a star rating that becomes visible when you hover over it in the store. Once you click into the product page, you’ll be able to read each review in more detail as you scroll down the page.
