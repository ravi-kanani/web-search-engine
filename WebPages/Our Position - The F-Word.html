Our Position

As contributors, we’ve all read things we don’t agree with on the site. We see this diversity of opinion as a strength. The F-Word is a vehicle for many intertwined but, crucially, individual feminist voices and not a faceless corporate machine. Our words are not ‘The F-Word View’. We do not speak as a site. We speak as writers for it.
