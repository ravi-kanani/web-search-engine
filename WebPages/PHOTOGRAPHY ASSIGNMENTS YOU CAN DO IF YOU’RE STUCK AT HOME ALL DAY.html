PHOTOGRAPHY ASSIGNMENTS YOU CAN DO IF YOU’RE STUCK AT HOME ALL DAY

Practical ideas:

1. Shoot 4K video on your camera (or 1080p), then play the video on your laptop or phone, and pause and shoot screenshots of whatever stills you like.

For example I watched this video in full-screen, and just took screenshots of any stills I liked:

2. Do 20 bodyweight pushups (right now) then photograph anything you see around the house which interests you.

Just shoot your food or whatever! These don’t gotta be “good” photos. Just have fun with it!

3. Shoot videos, then turn them into animated GIF’s

I really like ‘Giphy Capture‘ on the Mac. This is how I make my animated GIF’s from my videos.

4. Selfie project

Just shoot selfies of yourself at home. You don’t gotta share them with anyone. Your own face is the best face to shoot.

5. Curate your portfolio of your favorite photos

Look through all your old photos, and select your favorites. Upload them to your website/blog. Curating your photos means selecting the photos you CARE for!
