    The Skinny on PetSmart

    Our furry, scaly and feathered friends give us more than companionship; they enrich our lives. But as pet parents, we are the ones who must provide the love, care and sustenance they need to live happily and healthily. In order to help animal lovers do their very best, PetSmart provides the highest quality food and supplies for your animal companions. With more than 1600 stores across the USA, Canada and Puerto Rico, PetSmart is a trusted and familiar name.

    How do you use a PetSmart coupon?

    Once you’ve added everything to your cart, scroll down. You’ll need to enter your code in the ‘Promo Code’ box (just above the cart total) and hit ‘Apply’.

    After that, you can continue with the checkout process as normal by choosing how you want to pay.

    What should I know about shipping from PetSmart?

    Just like the pets themselves, PetSmart products come in all shapes and sizes, so shipping varies and is calculated with every order. In some cases, you’ll only be able to purchase products in-store.

    There is, however, free shipping on offer. To qualify, you’ll have to sign up to the PetSmart Treats reward program and spend over $49. If you meet the requirements, the free shipping discount will be applied automatically.

    What does my PetSmart Charities Donation go towards?

    When you checkout, near where your PetSmart promo code goes, you’ll find an option to donate to PetSmart charities.

    Any amount helps and every donation counts towards efforts that help find a lifelong, loving home for every pet. By funding adoption events and centers, pet rescue programs and support for spay/neuter efforts, your donation will help the estimated seven million pets in shelters to find a happier life.

    Does PetSmart accept returns?

    Of course! You won’t need to hound them for it either, with instructions outlined on the packing slip that comes with your shipped products.

    If you’d prefer to pop into a PetSmart store, they will happily handle your return. Simply bring the packing slip or the shipping confirmation email as proof of purchase.

    Whether you return in-store or otherwise, the product must be in its original packaging, and prescription medicines cannot be returned.

    How can I find my local PetSmart store?

    If your animal instincts haven’t already lead you there, you can always find one of PetSmart’s pet stores using their website.

    You’ll find a full listing of their locations across the USA, Canada and Puerto Rico, or you can simply enter your zip code to find a PetSmart near you.

    These PetSmart products look good. Can you show me reviews?

    Most PetSmart products will have customer reviews. Just take a leisurely scroll down the product page and you’ll find them in full detail.

    With such a large and passionate community of customers, you’ll be provided a balance of honest opinions from people that have actually bought and used the product.
