Qwixx™

A Fast Family Dice Game

This quick-playing dice game will have you on the edge of your seat from beginning to end! Qwixx is simple to play but each decision is crucial - the more numbers you cross off, the more points you score. With no downtime between turns you’ll have a chance to gain from each and every roll. Just one round of this thrilling game and you will be caught up in Qwixx-fever!

  • 8+
  • 2-5
  • 15 min

About the Game

We first discoverd this clever little dice game at the Essen game fair and it had us hooked after only one round of play. Who would have guessed there’d be so many exciting twists and turns in such a small game? We love how all players are involved with every roll of the dice. With simple rules, fast decision-making and thrilling risk-taking, Qwixx is sure to become a dice classic!
