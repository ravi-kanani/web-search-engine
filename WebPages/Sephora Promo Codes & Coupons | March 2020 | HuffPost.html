    The Skinny on Sephora

    Starting in Paris during the 70s, Sephora was born to be a revelation. The luxurious company’s innovative approach to beauty-retail has earnt it worldwide recognition and has totally changed the way we shop our beauty products.

    Whether you know your FENTY from your GUCCI or if you have no clue - you’re in the right place.

    Sephora is a veritable haven where makeup, beauty and fragrance fanatics can find cult products from talked-about brands. The largest luxury store in North America, we get up close and personal with the beauty experts.

    For new look inspo and makeup advice, are Sephora reviews available to read online?

    There are the Sephora community pages online where you’ll get the lowdown on products and more from fellow shoppers and Sephora beauty experts. This is a dedicated beauty Q&A section that is filled with advice and Sephora reviews from like-minded beauty fiends. Whether you’re reading or writing a review and rating a product yourself, this is a good place to be.

    How do you use a Sephora promo code?

    You’ll be able to enter your promo code at the checkout under the order summary. Just choose a promo code to get started then follow the pop-up instructions to shop Sephora online and add items to your cart.

    For events and Sephora makeup classes, are these always in Sephora stores?

    They are, and the calendar is full when it comes to makeup tutorials and expert classes. You’d be foolish to miss out on these events. Whether you need to figure out your foundation fit or finally nail your skincare, browse using your ZIP code, City or State online and find out where you can get your beauty 101.

    When you’ve found the right foundation and you’re ready to make that commitment, how much will Sephora regular shipping cost?

    Sephora offers free regular shipping on orders over $50 and it costs $5.95 otherwise. The total delivery time is 4 business days, but unlimited FLASH and priority shipping options are available. Check out the particular details online, including product restrictions.

    And are you able to track a Sephora order?

    You can sign in to your Sephora account and your order status will be visible under order history. You’ll find the tracking number to the right of your desired shipping address, click it for more detailed information. You can also look up your Sephora order online.

    What about when a product doesn’t work well or if you order the wrong item, can you return beauty items to Sephora for free?

    You can return items to any Sephora for free. You can take them to any Sephora store or use the pre-paid returns label supplied.

    And how often does the Sephora sale happen?

    To stay in the know about sales and beauty inspiration, it’s worth signing up to Sephora emails and following them on social media. You can do this from the Sephora.com homepage and you’ll find the social media links below.
