Sir William Turner (c, Attorney to the Duke of York)

  • Annotations (1)
  • References (4)

1 Annotation

✹

Terry Foreman on 4 Jun 2012  •  Link

Sir William Turner, kt 1664 (d. c. 1670). Civil lawyer; Fellow of Wadham College, Oxford 1628-37; advocate Doctors' Commons 1641; Judge of the Admiralty Court under the Commonwealth; M.P. for Bodmin Jan-Apr. 1679; Attorney to the Duke of York; Chancellor of Winchester diocese 1660-70.
