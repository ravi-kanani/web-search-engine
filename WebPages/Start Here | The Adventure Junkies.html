Welcome to The Adventure Junkies

The Adventure Junkies is the place to learn a new outdoor activity without any knowledge or previous experience required. By combining our actionable digital resources and our supportive online communities, we make getting out into nature possible for anyone. 

​

Follow the 2 steps below and hit the trail in no time!

1. Join The Community

One of the best parts of learning a new outdoor activity is the people you meet along the way. Join Summit and connect with other adventure junkies just like you. Ask questions, get help, share stories and help other people. Together we can do amazing things.

2. Download Our Guide

Learn how to plan and prepare for your next outdoor adventures with our FREE beginner's guides. These guidebooks have been written specifically for beginners. There's no techie language, just the essentials to get you outdoors as soon as possible. Download it today and start reading!
