Before you publish:

Think about it: the worksheet you're about to submit will be downloaded hundreds or even thousands of times. It will be printed out and handed to thousands of students in dozens of countries. It would be such a shame if a typo or a mistake slipped in (and we hate mistakes and typos just like you do).
We understand that you're superbusy, but please - pretty please! - take a few extra minutes proofreading your worksheet, its title and description. We have prepared some tips to make sure your worksheet is not rejected. Hover your mouse over the '?' symbol next to each field, and read a short note next to it, too.
