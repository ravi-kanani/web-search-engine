Sören von Malmborg is a Swedish mixing and mastering engineer. He has worked with artists and songwriters such as Avicii, Alan Walker, Kygo, NONONO, Seeb, Vargas & Lagola, Tove Lo, A$AP Rocky, Noonie Bao and Dagny.[1]

Sören worked with Alan Walker on his multi-platinum selling hit single "Faded",[2] which currently has over one billion streams on Spotify[3] and more than 2.3 billion views on YouTube.[4]
