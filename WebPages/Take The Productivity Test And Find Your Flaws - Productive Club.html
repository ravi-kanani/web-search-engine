Take The Productivity Test And Find Your Flaws

0shares

In this test you will be evaluated in multiple areas such as Working with Focus, defining long term goals, planning your day, punctuality, learning from mistakes, tracking your thoughts and more.

The most important part of this test is to be honest with your answers. Based on your answers, you will be see an analysis of areas where you could improve along with the things to do. Your intention here should not be get a high score by giving fake answers but to realize where you stand, improve and grow.
