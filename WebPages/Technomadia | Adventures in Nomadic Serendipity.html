Embracing Nomadic Serendipity

Welcome to our Virtual Living Room! We’re Chris Dunphy and Cherie Ve Ard (Technomadia)

We hit the road in 2006 and haven’t stopped since.

We combine technology and perpetual travel (tech+nomad) to create a sustainable mobile lifestyle.

We’ve had many substrates over the years – and currently have a nomadic fleet of RVs and a boat.

You've found our personal blog - where we share our travel adventures and stuff that interests us. We love sharing a slice of our nomadic life from the road & water (when our cat Kiki allows it) in written, photo and video formats.

Grab a tasty beverage and start exploring.. and please do say hi!

MobileInternetInfo.com

Mobile Internet options for RVing or boating?

We founded the Mobile Internet Resource Center in 2014 after fielding so many questions on this blog about how we kept connected to work remotely since we hit the road. 

It's now our full time 'day job' and our passion.

If you're seeking mobile internet solutions for your own mobile lifestyle, come join us in our office.
