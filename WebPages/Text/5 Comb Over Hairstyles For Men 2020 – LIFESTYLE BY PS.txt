comb-over style is here to stay and we're going to see this hairstyle trending in 2020 as well. comb over haircut is one of the coolest and looks great on almost anyone. there are many types of comb-over hairstyles which includes, fade comb over, comb over undercut, side part and many more. this hairstyle has a universal appeal to it. and you will men all over world sporting this cool haircut. especially, sweeping hair to the side. the good thing is no matter your face shape, age or hair type this hairstyle is super easy to do and it will look good on everyone. scroll below to check out 5 comb over hairstyles you can try in 2020. best comb over haircuts 2020 1. comb over fade comb over fade haircut features faded sides with classic side swept hair. it's easy to pull off and versatile. if you are a fan of fades and comb over style then this comb over fade style is perfect for you. ---------- ---------- 2. short comb over if you like to keep things simple, then this short comb over haircut is perfect for. basically, the length of your hair is gradual when you go from sides to top. a lot of men prefer to go for a short comb over haircut because it's easy to maintain to quick to set. 3. classic comb over the classic version of this haircut requires a tapered cut. you don't want to have sharp fades on the sides. and keep your top hair relatively shorter. just enough length so you can sweep then on the side. as the name suggests this hairstyle has been in fashion for ages. 4. undercut comb over hairstyle by combining comb over with men's undercut haircut you can achieve this cool and edgy hairstyle. the sides are kept very short to get the perfect undercut look and hair on the top is kept long. this undercut comb over hairstyle is perfect for you if you love to look edgy. 5. long comb over hairstyle to get the perfect long comb over style, you should avoid faded sides and keep medium to long hair on top depending on your preference. this hairstyle is
