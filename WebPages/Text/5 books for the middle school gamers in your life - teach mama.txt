i get it. believe me, i get it. but i am willing to bet big pokédollars that at least one of these books will catch your gamer’s fancy. here’s the skinny. . . 5 books for the middle school gamers in your life first and foremost, my suggestion to you is that you read these books before or along with your child. nothing—and i mean nothing!—will get your kid interested in reading about his or her favorite game than a parent who takes the time to read about it, too. and face it–we want our kids to talk to us about their interests, right? so pick up the book, mom or dad, and read. 1.) pokemon visual companion, by dk 2.) pokemon deluxe essential handbook, by scholastic (or if your kiddo is a minecraft fanatic, go with the essential minecraft set by scholastic!) 3.) guinness world records: gamer’s edition 2020 4.) fortnite: special newsweek edition 5.) warcross, by marie lu want to know what i say about each of these books?
