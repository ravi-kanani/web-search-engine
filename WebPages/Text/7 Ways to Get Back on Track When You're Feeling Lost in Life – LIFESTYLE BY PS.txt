search cart cart 7 ways to get back on track when you're feeling lost in life mar 25, 2020 for many, life is a struggle and it's easy to get off course. learn seven easy ways to get things back on track when you're feeling lost in life. according to one survey in 2017, the national well-being index dropped to 61.5. if you feel like your well-being and sense of purpose is lower than normal, you don’t have to despair. it can be difficult, but you can pull yourself out of it and bring meaning to your life again. for many, life is a struggle and it's easy to get off course. learn seven easy ways to get things back on track when you're feeling lost in life. 1. feel everything one of the most important things you need to make sure you do is to feel everything. sometimes when we feel lost, it’s because we numbed everything so that we can’t feel sadness or anger, but we also can’t feel happiness. you should sit back and observe everything you feel, both mental and physical. it is easier said than done because our first reaction when dealing with negative emotions is to ignore them. but you can’t get out of the hole and find the light at the end of the tunnel if you don’t walk through the dark tunnel first. your emotional state can be a compass in your life, and you have to learn how to listen to it and follow its direction. make sure you listen to your gut and your intuition because that rarely lies. if you need some more reassurance, you can always turn to astrology and numerology. you should also check out the numerology number meanings. 2. do a life audit another thing you can do to help yourself is to sit back and do a life audit. these are helpful ways to help you figure out what area of your life you need to focus on and correct. you can figure out if you need to fix your family relationships, friendships, romantic relationships, career, your health, finances, creativity, and even spirituality. when you need to get your life back on track, you should assess where you are right now in all of these areas and figure out where you want to go. there are all kinds of different versions that you can do, so you just need to find the one that works best for you. the overall goal of this life audit is to make sure you can clearly see where you throughout your life. it can also help give you a sense of control over your life. when you feel like things are feeling out of control, this can be a sign that you have to spend the time and check-in with yourself. 3. do what you love to help you regain some love for your life, you should follow what you were passionate about. when's the last time you remember doing something actually fun that wasn't a chore or for work? do you remember how easy it was and how well it flowed? it probably didn't seem to take up too much time because you actually enjoyed doing it. as we get older, we forget how amazing life can be because we become constantly bombarded with responsibilities. make sure that you make time for your passions, and you'll find your life will change. 4. find your confidence finding your confidence will also help you throughout your life. having confidence will determine how you show up to life every day. if you feel good about yourself, you're more likely to perform better, and if you perform better, you could feel more fulfilled. when it feels like things are going out of control, it may seem that being confident just isn't in the cards for you, but it's easier than you think. if you want to feel good about your body, try and commit to fitness. if you want to feel smarter, make sure you read and learn from mentors. 5. go outside your comfort zone if you are feeling lost, you may have gotten too comfortable with your life. if you're comfortable, it's very easy to get bored and lose yourself. that means you should step outside of your comfort zone and try something different. how can you change your routine? is there a new hobby you could try? maybe it's time to quit the job you hate and go find your dream job. you can't be afraid to take risks. try doing something that scares you. 6. go on an adventure sometimes stepping outside of your comfort zone means going on an adventure. whether it's just a day trip, going to a solitary retreat, or buying a ticket to somewhere out of the country, this will let you tap into your adventurous side again. everyone feels alive when they're on an adventure, and it'll give you a fresh perspective of the world as well. 7. enjoy time alone you should also make sure that you are making enough time for yourself. when you spend time with yourself, you need to make sure that you are actually focusing on yourself. time alone doesn't have to be lonely. just because you're alone doesn't mean you're lonely; it just means you're taking time to focus on yourself rather than others. life can get really busy, and if you don't take a break, it can really wear you down. make sure you dedicate a portion of each day to being alone, even if it's just for five minutes. stop feeling lost in life today! if you're feeling lost in life, these tips should help bring you back some happiness and fulfillment. if the feeling of being lost still continues, make sure you reach out to a mental health professional. if you enjoyed this article, make sure you check out our website for others just like it! tags: depression share share on
