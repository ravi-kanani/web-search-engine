dog lovers share on facebook share on twitter share on pinterest rachel crocetti writer shares share tweet pin living and working in new york, i spend a lot of time with headphones on. whether it be blocking out the groaning of other commuters on the subway or zoning out on a project at work. i’ve just recently starting switching over from music to podcasts and realized there are a ton of interesting and exciting shows specifically about dogs. check out some of the best dogtastic podcasts below: 1. can i pet your dog? a personal favorite of mine, this podcast never fails to make me laugh on my commute or at my desk at work. every tuesday, dog owner allegra ringo, dog-wanter renee colvert, and their producer travis mcelroy chat about dogs in the news and, most importantly, about dogs they’ve met (or pet!) in the past week. every episode they have a special guest come on their show and they also have a segment called mutt minute where they spend a minute telling you everything there is to know about a certain breed. what is there not to love?! 2. dr. dunbar’s iwoof podcasts this podcast is perfect for teaching you all you need to know about dogs. there are five whole seasons of episodes and they cover everything from dogs who eat poop to viral dog videos. 3. creative dog training creative dog training is a podcast that specializes in filling you in on everything you need to know regarding owning a dog. their library is easily searchable by topic, from dog parks to vet visits. if you have a question about your pup, check this series first for an easy answer. 4. canine nation this podcast goes a little deeper into the behavioral science in dogs. posted monthly, the host eric brad has covered topics like mark and reward training and traveling with your dog. this is a great opportunity to hear exactly how your pup works, while also hearing personal stories about the host’s dog. 5. the dog trainer quick and dirty tricks this podcast, easily found on itunes, is pretty much exactly what it sounds like. if you have a question about your dog, this podcast will likely be able to answer it. whether talking about puppy trouble, or how to evaluate a dog walker, this is a great find. 6. dogcast radio this bimonthly podcast is essentially a radio talk show all about dogs. there are interviews with professionals, product reviews, information on specific breeds, and so much more. 7. it’s a doggy dog world this podcast by pet life radio posts weekly talk shows and has had big-time celebrities like betty white and rachael ray on their show! the hosts, petra burke, liz palika, and kate abbott talk about dog breeds, behaviors and give tidbits about their own dogs. 8. vegas rock dog radio sam “the queen of rock ‘n’ roll dogs” ratcliffe-d’arrigo broadcasts her podcast live from las vegas weekly. she talks about pets, people, and pop culture surrounding the two and she often brings on cool guests and experts on all-things-pets. featured image
