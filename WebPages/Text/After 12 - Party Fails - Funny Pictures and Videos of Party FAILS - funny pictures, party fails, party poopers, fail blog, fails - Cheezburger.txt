university of illinois at chicago pegged the ideal blood alcohol content for creativity at 0.075 percent. that level is known as the creative peak, and may well be the ideal state for problem solving, inventing and general "out of the box" thinking. using that data, crispin porter + bogusky's copenhagen office has come up with the perfect beer for creatives—an ipa called the problem solver. there's nothing too special about the beer itself. instead, it's the label that will help you reach the magical 0.075 percent mark—it shows you how of the much of the bottle you need to drink to get there,
