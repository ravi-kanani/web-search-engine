search cart cart australian veganism: how to transition well in the plant-based lifestyle mar 25, 2020 australia is synonymous to its beef and meat products. however, more australians are now opting to turn into plant-based diets. new reports claim that there are almost 2.5 million aussies who adapted a vegetarian or mostly vegetarian lifestyle in 2018. meanwhile, about one per cent of the entire population in the country classify themselves as vegans. these people would wear vegan shoes australia and other clothing items not made using animal products. if you want to switch to a straightforward vegan lifestyle, here are several tips that you must follow. determine your reason why you want to become a vegan according to the original definition published by the vegan society, the vegan way of life seeks to eliminate all types of animal cruelty and exploitation. it means that they choose to stop patronising any food items, clothing, and other products that harm the animals. if this is the same reason why you want to transition in this lifestyle, then you will have no problems embracing it for life. by definition, vegetarianism is the lifestyle of people who opted to stop consuming meat, poultry, or fish. on the other hand, veganism refers to a lifestyle wherein people choose to stay away from animal and animal by-products like eggs, honey, and dairy. aside from this, vegans refuse to use items made using animal by-products like cosmetics, soaps, accessories, and vegan shoes in australia. people do this to pay respect to the animals. learn how to survive without consuming animals the next step to help you become a certified vegan is to find out how to remain healthy even without meat consumption. know how to keep your body nourished without eating prime australian beef. find out how to receive all the essential vitamins and nutrients like vitamin b12 so you will remain healthy while on a vegan diet. then learn how to start living and eating without any animal products. it is inevitable to have a hard time shifting from your old lifestyle to a vegan way of life. but by researching proper vegan meal planning, shopping for vegan food, and looking for easy-to-cook vegan recipes, it would be less complicated to start the vegan lifestyle. ask for support from other vegans to have more guidance about your new way of life, you should contact other vegans in your area and ask them to become your support group. you can easily find one online or research for the extensive list of vegans’ groups in australia. it will also help if you join their regular activities to know more about how to make the vegan transition less complicated. they will also teach you where to find the best vegan restaurants in the market. being vegan is more than just eating a plant-based diet. it is more about practising the ideology where animals have the right to be adequately treated since they also have lives of their own. if you practice all these tips, you
