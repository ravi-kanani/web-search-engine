strap, wrist strap pro, shoulder strap, chroma) and eric kim collection (wrist strap, neck strap) are 100% leather and handmade and crafted with care from start to finish. each henri strap is hand-made by two of our friends, lan and uyen who are a leather craftsmen and artists in saigon. eric and i are their clients and collaborators, and we worked together with them to design the strap. since they are our friends and collaborators, they have always set the price for the straps to cover their costs and labor. lan and uyen source the leather, handmake the straps during the weekdays between music gigs, and ship them to california from saigon. rather than mass produced, these are handmade in small, limited-quantity batches in order to preserve a level of excellent craftsmanship
