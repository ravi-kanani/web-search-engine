  scroll-margin-top: 5rem; /* whatever is a nice number that gets you past the header */
} we have an almanac article on it, which includes browser support, which is essentially everywhere. it's often talked about in conjunction with scroll snapping, but i find this use case even more practical. here's a simple demo: codepen embed fallback in a related vein, that weird (but cool) "text fragments" link that chrome shipped takes you to the middle of the page instead, which i think is nice. our learning partner need some front-end development training?
