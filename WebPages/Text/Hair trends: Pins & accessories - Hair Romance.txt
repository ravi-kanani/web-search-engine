trends: pins & accessories march 6, 2020 by hair romance leave a comment time for a round-up of my fave hair accessories right now! plus a heads up, the shopbop sale is on now so you can save with the code spring. hair pins and barrettes are still having their moment in the spotlight and i am here for it. if you’re having a hair struggle, adding an accessory is an instant way to lift your look. your accessory will draw all the attention and your hair troubles will be forgotten. this classic circle clip is such a simple way to dress up a ponytail for everyday or for an event. scarves and headbands add colour to a plain outfit and i have a range of vintage scarves. this new trend for shaped headbands combines millinery and hair accessories together. this namjosh embellished headband instantly elevates a low bun or ponytail. it’s great for frizzy hair too as the design of the headband really stands out and helps to shape your hair. it wouldn’t be hair romance without some love in the hair! i’m always on the lookout for cute heart accessories but there’s a fine line between cute and cutesy. this mother of pearl heart is simple and sophisticated. it also looks fantastic in dark hair as it really contrasts and stands out. local aussie indie brand, husskie, has recently launched the most incredible collection of accessories. i’m trying to decide which beaded bag to get and these tortoiseshell rectangular barrettes are just stunning. classic for everyday and a chic way to elevate your hairstyle. and because trends come and go, but classics are forever, this panama hat is a winner in my eyes. a sophisticated hat is my fallback for a bad hair day, and it’s also sun safe too. with a smaller hat like this madewell panama, you don’t have to take it off when you’re inside so no-one will ever find out what your hair really looks like underneath. how’s your hair accessory game going right now? what are you wearing in your hair? shopbop advertises on hair romance and it’s
