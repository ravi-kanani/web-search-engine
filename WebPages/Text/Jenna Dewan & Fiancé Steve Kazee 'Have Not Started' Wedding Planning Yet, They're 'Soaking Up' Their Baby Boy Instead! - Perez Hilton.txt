home › jenna dewan › jenna dewan & fiancé steve kazee ‘have not started’ wedding planning yet, they’re ‘soaking up’ their baby boy instead! jenna dewan and steve kazee are soaking up all the newborn goodness their new arrival has to offer! as we previously shared, the step up star and her fiancé welcomed her son callum michael rebel kazee on march 6, but don’t expect a wedding to come anytime soon! related: jenna dewan dishes on her ‘peaceful’ and ‘calm’ birth experience aside from the fact that we’re literally dealing with a global pandemic so it’s not feasible to get hitched right now, the couple says their main priority is their baby boy. dewan told people: “we have not started any planning yet. right now we’re enjoying our new life of parenthood and soaking up this gorgeous newborn. we’ll get there when we get there.” the 39-year-old also reflected on how california’s “safer at home” order has impacted the past few weeks since welcoming their son: “i was planning to nest at home and spend time and really good quality time with our new baby, so not much of that has changed. the difference is we’re not having any visitors.” but that’s totally fine with them! the momma of two mused about how steve is doing as a first-time dad: “it comes so natural to him and he’s really embraced it and he’s just incredible. and it doesn’t hurt that our wonderful son gets to hear his lullabies.” super cute!! she added that she knew the tony award winner would be an “incredible” dad to their child after seeing him interact with her daughter. as you’re likely aware, jenna shares 6-year-old daughter everly rose tatum with her ex-husband channing tatum. she explained: “i knew he had this gift because i’ve gotten to see him be incredible with my daughter. but seeing him with a newborn and the gentleness he has. just watching him look into callum’s eyes and sing to him and hold him. he really just embraced it in a way that like i was pretty blown away by.” throughout her pregnancy, kazee shared more than a few mushy (but adorable) posts about his baby momma, which were always so sweet to see! and he took things to the next level when he popped the question to dewan at her baby shower back in february. she told the outlet: “i had no idea. he started singing this song and at the end said ‘will you marry me?’ all of my closest friends were there, it was such a special moment. apparently evie was in on this for months and didn’t tell me, which is adorable.” so cute!
