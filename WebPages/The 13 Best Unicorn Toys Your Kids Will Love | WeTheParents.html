Buyers' Guide

What are some things I should consider when choosing a great unicorn toy for my child?

  • Age appropriateness: Whenever you’re choosing clothes, toys, or gear for your kiddo, making sure it’s appropriate for their age is important. For younger kids, choosing age-appropriate toys can help to avoid choking hazards or complex games or puzzles that might be frustrating. Likewise, older kids won’t enjoy the same toys as their younger counterparts, and might get bored by a game, toy, or book that’s not designed for someone their age.
  • Safe, high quality materials: There are a LOT of unicorn toys out there, and as is the case with many popular themed toys, some picks are better than others. Look for clothes, toys, and games that are made out of safe, durable material like non-toxic BPA-free plastic, stainless steel, or cotton, among others; a good rule of thumb is that off-brand toy prices that seem to be too good to be true generally are, so looking to brands with safety standards you trust is often your best bet.
  • Their unique personality: Your child has been showing their unique personality to you from the moment of their birth, and you’re one of the people who knows them best of all. The best pick isn’t always the most obvious, like a stuffed animal that an older child may not get much use of. The unicorn theme has transcended toydom into popular culture and is frequently spotted on colorful accessories like water bottles, binders, backpacks, and hoodies that teens can both enjoy and use.

Why are unicorn toys great for kids?

As a fantasy creature, unicorns can inspire creativity and imagination. That makes them an excellent toy for use in pretend play, acting as characters and elements of storytelling and make-believe role playing. This kind of play isn’t just fun for younger kids, it’s developmentally essential, as it helps them grasp basic social skills and develop both empathy and theory of mind.

For older kids, they’re a symbol of free-spirited creativity beloved by many spunky, colorful preteens and teens who think outside the box. Wearing and using unicorn-themed clothes and accessories is sometimes a part of their self-expression at a time when assertion of their identity is a huge part of how they establish feelings of self assurance and confidence.

My child loves unicorns, but they’re too old to really enjoy or use a stuffed animal. What are some unicorn gifts they’d enjoy?

  • Clothes: Whether it’s hoodies, bathrobes, t-shirts, or pajama pants, there are lots of comfy and cute unicorn clothes available for kids of all ages
  • Puzzles: For kids who like a mental challenge, a unicorn jigsaw puzzle can offer a way to boost their logical thinking and problem solving skills with a satisfying project that can either be completed again or matted and framed
  • Games: There are a surprising amount of family-friendly and fun unicorn-adjacent card and board games out there, which are great for stimulating creative and logical thinking and encouraging time spent socially with family and/or friends
  • Accessories: Headbands, headphones, necklaces, bracelets, backpacks, and more can all be found in a bold, colorful, and shiny unicorn theme, and for a little fashionista, they’re a great way to let her express her personal style.
