The Cone By Wiski: A Modern Cat Scratcher & Bed

Posted on March 6, 2017 by Tamar

If you’re a regular reader of this blog you know my belief that making a home cat friendly and expressing one’s design aesthetic do not have to be mutually exclusive. And I love finding and sharing products and tips that help achieve a cat home that makes all it’s residents comfortable! And living in NYC where apartments are on the small side, multi purpose space-saving products are always something I’m looking for.

So when Evan Ryan – a cat man and industrial designer – shared his new product The Cone by Wiski with me, I was immediately intrigued and had to share it with you. Evan designed The Cone because, “He found himself struggling to find products that fit his cat’s needs as well as his design sensibilities” (sound familiar?!).

Things I love about the Cone:

  • Contemporary design
  • Compact design (perfect for tucking into a corner)
  • Dual purpose (scratcher and bed)
  • Replaceable 100% natural sisal cover
  • Machine washable cushion cover

And even though it’s tall, it’s sturdy because it’s supported by solid steel rods that also makes the base.

The Cone will be available through a Kickstarter this spring, and I hear other color combinations are in the works! While there’s no word on pricing yet, if you love the look of this product as much as I do, be sure to sign up for the mailing list NOW to be alerted to early bird discounted pricing!

I hope The Cone is a huge success because I can’t wait to see what other sleek modern designs Evan has up his sleeve!  Do you love this design as much as I do?! What other color combos would you like to see?
