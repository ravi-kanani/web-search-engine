What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization, or SEO for short, is a marketing-related discipline which focuses on increasing visibility in organic search engine results. It is used for driving traffic, improving rankings, and increasing recognition of your newly created website in search engines.

Ultimately, SEO is about ensuring that your website is structured and designed in a way that search engines will understand and thus make it easier for people to find you. The first and most important step to achieving that is coming up with the right keywords – the ones that are relevant for the content of your website, and the ones that have low competition and a high search volume.

Apart from crafting your content and finding the appropriate keywords, you need to optimize your code and build links, and the best way to do that is by incorporating SEO tools.

Why do you need SEO tools?

If you implement the right SEO tools, your website will be able to rank high in searches, thus acquiring higher visibility and more targeted traffic – a higher ranking directly leads to more visitors. These powerful tools can help you improve your online presence, get a higher ROI, and bring more profit in the long run.

You want to create quality content based on keywords that rate high, but identifying them will require time consuming and extensive research. Using the right SEO tools can save you a lot of valuable time when it comes to keyword research.

In addition, they will help you optimize your content, so that you can gain credibility and authority on the web. Moreover, SEO tools enable you to closely study your competition in order to make sure that you are always one step ahead of them.

List of the most popular SEO tools

Now that we have covered the benefits of using these tools, it’s time to go over some of the best options out there. Here is a list of highly effective and popular SEO tools that will help you transform your website:

Ahrefs

Ahrefs.com is a well-known toolset for backlinks and SEO analysis. Most people think of Ahrefs as a powerful “backlink checker” tool. But in reality, “checking backlinks” is just one of the many things that you can do with their huge data index.

Visit Ahrefs

SEMrush

SEMrush is a tool that is specific to finding profitable keywords. Many new and older bloggers are using it to optimize their sites, create the content their audience wants and create a better experience for their visitors.

Visit SEMrush

KWFinder

KWFinder is an easy-to-use keyword research tool bringing perfect keyword ideas. It helps you find long tail keywords with low SEO difficulty.

Visit KWFinder

Screaming Frog SEO Spider

The SEO Spider is a desktop program/website crawler, that allows you to crawl websites’ URLs and fetch key onsite elements to analyse onsite SEO.

Visit Screaming Frog SEO Spider

Moz Pro

Moz Pro is MOZ’s all-in-one suite of SEO tools. It ties together keyword research metrics by collecting data on websites across four search engines to provide you with SEO analytics and insights.

Visit Moz Pro

Google Search Console

Google Search Console is a free service offered by Google that helps you monitor and maintain your site’s presence in Google Search results.

Visit Google Search Console

LongTail Pro

Long Tail Pro is a keyword research tool for long tail keywords that helps you discover thousands of profitable, targeted keywords and calculate Keyword Competitiveness for almost any niche.

Visit LongTail Pro

Google Keyword Planner

Google AdWords Keyword Planner is a keyword research tool that allows you to find the rightkeywords to target for display ads, search ads, video ads, and app ads.

Visit Google Keyword Planner

Wordtracker

Wordtracker is a free keyword research tool. You can save your keyword lists, filter them in the tool and find the keywords you need for every page all in one place.

Visit Wordtracker

Linkbird

Linkbird is a Content Marketing Software for more online visibility via Content Marketing, SEO, Link Building and Online PR.

Visit Linkbird

WooRank

SEO Checker & Instant Website Reviewer you can use to analyze and optimize unlimited websites.

Visit WooRank

SE Ranking

SE Ranking is a fully automated universal Google rankings checker with 100% accurate results.

Visit SE Ranking

MarketGoo

DIY SEO tool great for small businesses – includes a Free SEO Report and a 10 day free trial of the paid SEO tool that guides you in optimizing your site for better search engine rankings and more traffic.

Visit MarketGoo

Raven

Raven provides detailed SEO audit reports that show progress over time, track your ranking changes on SERPs to see if those onsite changes did the job. Identify potential backlink sources, manage and report your progress.

Visit Raven

Netpeak Spider

Netpeak Spider is a desktop tool which crawls your website like a search engine robot and detects key SEO issues that influence the website’s visibility in SERP.

Visit Netpeak Spider

SEO PowerSuite

SEO PowerSuite is an all-in-one toolkit that covers every step of an SEO campaign of any caliber, from rankings and website audit to backlink research and link building. With its intuitive UI and a wealth of pro-level features, it’s a perfect fit for SEO newbies and experts alike.

Visit SEO PowerSuite

Serpstat

Serpstat is a toolkit for SEO & PPC keyword research, backlink analysis, rank tracking, keyword clustering, and more. Its keyword and competitor analysis data cover all the Google regions in the world.

Visit Serpstat

Pulno

Pulno is a toolset for SEO analysis. It provides detailed SEO audits with such parameters as page speed, meta descriptions, duplicate content check, backlinks, keyword analysis and many more.

Visit Pulno

Conclusion

If you want your website to really be successful, using SEO tools is a must. So, after learning about some of the basics and the importance of SEO, be sure to try the above-mentioned tools which are guaranteed to help you meet your objectives.
