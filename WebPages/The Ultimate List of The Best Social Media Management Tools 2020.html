What is social media?

Social media is a popular term used for large sites where users can participate in various types of social activities. For example, LinkedIn is a business social network where professionals connect with each other, look to establish themselves as experts, find jobs, look for employees, engage B2B partnerships and so on.

On the other hand, Facebook is a general social network where people can do pretty much anything, from sharing status updates, videos, photos, join groups, and many other things. All of these activities which people take part in on social media open up many opportunities for advertising.

However, the most important element is that in which people look to discover new content and view things on social media. This is why a lot of companies were successful in engaging people on social media and fuel their business efforts.

Why do you need social media management tools?

Once you start a website, you have to be present on multiple social media platforms to be able to reach as many people from their target audiences as possible. In these situations, webmasters and companies are unable to manage, update, and keep track of all of their profiles and not to mention doing proper analytics.

This is where social media management (SMM) tools come into play. Not only that these tools allow you to manage all of your social media profiles on different platforms from a single dashboard, but they also have great features for accurate analytics, scheduled posts, and tracking impressions.
