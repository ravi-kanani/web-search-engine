Top 23 Realtor WordPress Themes For Business Or Real Estate Listing Type Websites 2020

  • January 20, 2020
  • Aigars
  • Theme Collections
  • 2 Comments

Few things are as important as your home. Considering this simple truth, it becomes obvious that the job of a realtor is very impactful. It is based on persuasion, trust, and an eye for detail. Given the stakes, your clients will be very cautious and they will analyze every word, action, and gesture. Your character and presentation can be nothing short of impeccable, and your website must reflect that quality as well.

Due to the wonders of the internet, people from across the planet can access your page, and learn everything about your company. Although potential customers will spend just a few seconds browsing, their first impression will be informed by what they see. Your realtor page must be gorgeous, functional and informative.

There are many WordPress themes that seamlessly blend practicality and aesthetic appeal, resulting in a site that inspires trust and professionalism. Dependable and cheap, themes can eliminate the need to hire a web development firm. Many of them can be installed with a single click, and their menus are intuitive and streamlined. They maximize convenience without sacrificing versatility, offering a well-rounded experience for all realtor websites.

Here are the best realtor WordPress themes to boots your sales.

Webify


Webify is a highly promising realtor WordPress theme with all the necessary layouts and elements. With fifty and counting demos, you know that Webify is a serious deal. Of course, it also has an exclusive design for real estate and realtor needs. With a clean and modern appearance, the chances are high that you would want to use the sample out of the box. You can just replace the contents with your details and information and enter the online space already. Working with a tool, like Webify, unlocks everyone a broad horizon of possibilities.

When pushing properties online like a champ, Webify will surely do the trick for you. Instead of starting from scratch, save yourself a tremendous amount of time and energy. You do not even need to be a pro coder nor designer to be able to succeed at establishing a realtor website with Webify.

Divi

Divi is an excellent WordPress theme that can improve your website. It incorporates a large number of practical features, implemented in order to attract more customers. With this product, anyone can create a successful business page. No technical expertise or programming knowledge is required. In terms of customization, you are able to design your own layout from scratch. However, if time is scarce, customers can also implement one of the 18 pre-made page layouts that are included in the download. They can act as templates for how a page should be organized, inspiring further modification and improvement.

Although many products claim to be innovative, Divi is actually pushing some boundaries.  Every single screen element will be represented by basic modules called blocks. These blocks make it very easy for you to customize your page via the Divi Builder. Customers can completely alter their site’s appearance in a matter of moments, crafting something that matches their original vision. This is one of the best-looking themes on the market, and its versatility is staggering. It can adapt to almost any business niche, and it is entirely responsive. Indeed, your content can be easily displayed on mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. For more information about Divi theme you can read our detailed review.

Real Places

For your realtor website, Real Places is an ideal choice. This WordPress theme has a niche-specific design and it can also be flexible. Various requirements can be easily fulfilled, due to its sizable roster of practical features.  Of course, your website can incorporate a powerful search function with various criteria. You are able to select which fields are displayed, and filter results in terms of location or price. Basically, Real Places hands you the reins of your site, as every aspect will be under your control.

Home page modules can be customized at any time, due to the implementation of drag and drop technology. Just as the name suggests, you only need to select an element and drag it across the screen. It is also possible to use the WPBakery Page Builder.  There are 3 distinct design versions for your property detail page. You can shift between them by accessing the Theme Options section. Real Places has 3 distinct page templates that can showcase agents in 2-4 column layouts. You can now prevent botting and spam accounts, thanks to the integrated Google ReCaptcha support. For those who prefer a more hands-on demonstration, a live theme preview wad added. There is an HTML version available for this theme which you can find here.

It should also be mentioned that no specialized knowledge is required. Your content can be viewed on high resolution Retina displays and most web browsers.
