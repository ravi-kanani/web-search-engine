EST 2012

Hi, I’m Shing from the UK and I live in London. Welcome to my travel blog – The Culture Map. You’ll find tips, guides, recommendations and the odd misadventure from my travels that span over 50 countries and 6 continents.

I’ve written about many places, but in my archive there’s a strong focus on Scandinavia and the Arctic. It may be chilly up there, but my gosh, it’s beautiful!

Heading North
Scandinavia & the Arctic Regions

I’ve travelled extensively throughout Scandinavia and I’m hopelessly in love with it. People often have many questions about this region, including the area that lies above the Arctic Circle. This blog attempts to answer them.

Norway

Probably the world’s most beautiful country. Experience Norway’s dramatic coastline, towering waterfalls and mountain peaks. Read more…

Sweden

Sweden has a reputation for beautiful people, but the country herself is a stunner too! Explore the Western coastline, the cool capital of Stockholm, or where not head further up North where the hotels get quirkier and quirkier? Read more…

Denmark

Experience the laid-back energy of Copenhagen, the stylish architecture of Aarhus and the chance to explore the stunning coastline and countryside of this small but perfectly formed country. Read more…
