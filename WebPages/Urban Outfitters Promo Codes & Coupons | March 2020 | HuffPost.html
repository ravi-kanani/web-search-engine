    The Skinny on Urban Outfitters

    The mighty Urban Outfitters has grown exponentially from its humble roots in a small retail space opposite the University of Pennsylvania to become a popular lifestyle brand, synonymous with uber-cool fashion and trending home style. From hand-crafted homeware and luxurious bed linen to graphic tees and distressed denim, only the quirkiest, quality products make it into the Urban Outfitters store.

    With an aim to inspire its customers through physical experiential environments as well as an extensive online offering, Urban Outfitters is renowned as a one-stop shop for the modern creative’s fashion, beauty and entertainment needs.

    How do I use my Urban Outfitters promo code?

    Copy the offer code if one is provided and follow the link to browse the Urban Outfitters store. Fill your bag with goodies and proceed to the checkout page. Here, you can paste your code into the appropriate box to redeem your discount.

    Does Urban Outfitters offer free shipping?

    US customers are in luck! Urban Outfitters offers free standard shipping (5-7 business days) with all orders of $50 or over. Orders below this value will cost $4.95 to ship.

    However, if you can’t wait that long for your parcel, not to worry! You can choose the express shipping option (2-3 business days) for $14.96 or rush shipping (1-2 business days) for $21.95.

    Does Urban Outfitters accept returns?

    Whether you bought your Urban Outfitters products in-store or online, you can return unworn, unwashed items or defective merchandise for a refund or exchange within 30 days of delivery. If you miss this window, you can return your product in return for e-merchandise credit. For more details, please see the Urban Outfitters returns policy.

    Is it possible to track my Urban Outfitters order?

    Keep your eye on your Urban Outfitters package by logging into your order status with your order number and billing ZIP code to hand.

    Where can I find the Urban Outfitters size guide?

    Take a look at the Urban Outfitters size guides online to check your measurements before making a purchase. It’s the best way to ensure a perfect fit.
