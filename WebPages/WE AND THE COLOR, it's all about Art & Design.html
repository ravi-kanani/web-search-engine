WE AND THE COLOR, it’s all about Art & Design

What we are at WE AND THE COLOR…

WE AND THE COLOR was founded by Dirk Petzold in 2010 as a project dedicated to promote the most outstanding creative ideas of artists and designers from around the world. You can call it an art & design blog, a web magazine for the creative crowd or whatever. We want to inspire people and show that creativity has no bounds. With a focus on diverse fields such as art, design, illustration, photography, architecture, fashion, animation and filmmaking, WE AND THE COLOR is a website for professionals as well as people intested in art and design. In addition to the abundance of inspiration that you can find on WE AND THE COLOR, we also present useful tools and gadgets for the creative crowd such as fonts, graphics, books, website themes, templates or mockups.

With a degree as Designer for Digital & Print Media, Dirk Petzold started his professional career as freelance graphic designer and illustrator back in 2004. Through countless projects for clients from different business areas, he gained experience in the fields of Creative Direction, Branding, Advertising, Illustration, Graphic Design for Web and Print, etc. In May 2010, he has started WE AND THE COLOR as an art & design project on Tumblr. The site received a lot of attention and it quickly became one of the most popular design blogs on Tumblr. In 2012, WE AND THE COLOR was relaunched as an independent website.

If you would like to contact us or if you want to submit a project for review, please write to: meandyou@weandthecolor.com
