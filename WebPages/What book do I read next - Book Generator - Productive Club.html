FAQs on What Book Should I Read Next Generator

What is this what book do I read next generator?

Every book lover goes through the dilemma of which book to read next.  Sometimes you would go through Amazon and GoodReads to check a book in the category you love. You might perform random searches, glance through books similar to a book, look up kindle unlimited books, pick some books with good ratings and reviews and whatnot. You end up confused and frustrated many a time.

This generator solves the problem for all book lovers by picking a random book that is known to be good based on ratings and reviews. Just refresh the page and you will get a new book every time.

How do I find a new book to read in a category?

On this page, you have a section called - Generate a random book. You can pick one or more categories of your liking and you will get recommendations only from those categories.

What if I do not like the book suggested?

Just refresh the page or filter by the category in the section - Generate a random book.

How do you decide which books to recommend?

The books are handpicked based on what other users are saying about it on various platforms like Amazon and Good Reads. You can be sure that any book recommended has been liked by many other book lovers. But again, not everyone has the same taste so you might not like every single one.

In the future, the recommendations will be based on your taste in books.

Are new books added to the list?

New books are added to the list every week. By the time you read the book suggested by the What book should I read next generator, you will already have new suggestions waiting for you.

Why was the tool What book do I read next generator created?

Here is the story behind why I created this book generator. I am an avid book reader who reads over 50 books a year.

I have many friends who want to cultivate a habit of reading. They come to me asking, “What book do I read next?”

I would continue with a follow-up question asking, “What type of book would you like to read?” Some would say a novel, some wanted to read a self-help book, some wanted to learn entrepreneurship and what not.

I would then suggest then what book to read next based on my previous reading. Eventually, I thought since many people have this problem, why not build a tool to recommend the book to read next.

I now make it a practice to collate data of various books that provide value and update the list frequently. I ensure that the books recommended provide good value for the time spent reading.

Not every book recommended by the tool might wow you. But I am sure, you will not feel that you picked a useless book. Each book will provide some value for the time you spent.

If you are not a book lover, read the article on the unknown importance of reading books.

Happy Reading! I hope this solves your confusion about what book should you read next once and for all.
