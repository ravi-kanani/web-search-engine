Which of Your Photos Do You Truly Care For?

To curate your photos means to choose the photos you care for.

So friend– which photos do you truly care for?

1.Who do you love?

I love Cindy. I admire her. I respect her. Thus I photograph her. With her, I made CINDY PROJECT.

You cannot fake love. You cannot fake enthusiasm.

When you photograph those who you love and care for, there is a deeper level of intimacy.

2. Do you care for yourself?

I shoot selfies. Why? Because I love and respect myself. I consider myself beautiful, and I treat myself like my own photo project.

Furthermore, to photograph myself is a memento mori of some kind. I know I will die (hopefully by age 120), but I might die tonight or tomorrow — who knows?
