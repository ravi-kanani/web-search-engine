Blue Sky support offers industry-leading WordPress expert support. If you're getting started with WordPress we help with the basics, such as selecting a theme, making simple edits or setting up a contact form. If your WordPress site is up and running, we'll help you reach its full potential.

How do I get started with Blue Sky?

As you start a WordPress site, Blue Sky has different ways to interact with our WordPress experts. For simple questions, Blue Sky Lite provides email support. Get started by choosing your plan from the Bluehost Marketplace in your customer dashboard.

As a premiere WordPress setup service, our in-house WordPress developers contribute directly to the WordPress core code. We have years of experience building and hosting WordPress websites plus a team of highly-trained WordPress experts. Bluehost has been recommended by WordPress.org since 2005.
