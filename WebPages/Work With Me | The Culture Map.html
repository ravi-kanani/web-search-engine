Work With Me

Thank you for your interest in working with The Culture Map.

The Culture Map is a popular travel and culture blog written and curated by Shing, a female traveller with a degree in English Literature and a penchant for experiencing new things. The Culture Map appeals to a wide audience, from career professionals looking to capitalise upon their short holidays away from work to retirees looking to finally set sail on their lifelong dream.

Why work with The Culture Map?

  • Established Blog

The Culture Map has been around since 2012 and has been featured in The Guardian, Wanderlust, Rolling Stone Magazine and several other high profile media outlets. The blog continues to grow its audience and portfolio of destinations.

  • Experienced

Shing has over 8 years’ experience in the luxury travel sector working in both Product Development and Marketing. This means she has the unique perspective of knowing what travel companies require when they work with travel bloggers.

  • Creative

If you’re looking for someone to enhance your brand, Shing can curate a campaign to suit your needs or work with you to breath new life into your brand. Previous brand partnerships have included Thomas Cook Airlines, Withlocals and various tourism boards
