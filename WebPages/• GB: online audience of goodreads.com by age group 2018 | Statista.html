Distribution of the online audience of goodreads.com in GB 2018, by age group

Published by J. Johnson, Feb 14, 2020
This statistic displays the distribution of the online audience of goodreads.com in Great Britain (GB) in 2018, by age group. In 2018, 25 percent of the online audience of goodreads.com was aged between 25 and 34 years.
