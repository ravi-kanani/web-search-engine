Leading digital content services ranked by usage in the United Kingdom (UK) 2018

Published by J. Johnson, Sep 17, 2019
This statistic displays the leading digital content services in the United Kingdom (UK) ranked by usage among digital content consumers as of May 2018. YouTube ranked first, with 54 percent of respondents reporting having used the service to consume or share digital content within the three months previous to the survey.
