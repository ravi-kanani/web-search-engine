Italy: online video watching frequency 2018, by age group

Published by Daniela Coppola, Nov 14, 2019
This statistic displays the frequency in online video watching in Italy in 2018, by age group. According to survey results, 70 percent of individuals aged between 18 and 24 years old used to watch online videos more than once a day. Besides, 13 percent of respondents aged over 45 years old stated that they watched online videos few times per month, overrating all other age groups.
