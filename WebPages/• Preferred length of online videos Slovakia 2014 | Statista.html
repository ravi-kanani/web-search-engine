Slovakia: preferred length of online videos 2014

Published by Statista Research Department, Oct 26, 2014
This statistic shows data on the preferred length of online video content in Slovakia in 2014. During the survey period it was found that 27 percent of Slovakian internet users had recently watched a video that was more than 10 minutes long.
