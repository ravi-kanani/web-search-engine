UK: share of users posting comments on websites or blogs 2014-2015, by age group

Published by Statista Research Department, Apr 21, 2016
This statistic displays the findings of a survey on the share of internet users who contributed comments to a website or blog at least weekly in the United Kingdom (UK) in 2014 and 2015, by age group. During the survey period in 2014, it was found that 19 percent of responding internet users reported posting comments on websites or blogs at least weekly.
