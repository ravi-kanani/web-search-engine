YouTube use to consume or share digital content in the United Kingdom (UK) 2012-2018

Published by J. Johnson, Feb 14, 2020
This statistic presents the share of legal digital content consumers that used YouTube to consume and/or share digital content in the United Kingdom (UK) from 2012 to 2018. As of the most recent survey wave, ending in March 2018, 58 percent of respondents reported using YouTube for this purpose.

Use of YouTube to consume or share digital content among legal digital content consumers in the United Kingdom (UK) from 2012 to 2018

Share of respondents
March 2018 53%
March 2017 48%
March to May 2016 46%
March to May 2015 41%
March to May 2013 42%
November 2012 to January 2013 39%
August to October 2012 42%

Supplementary notes

*** Content types included: music, e-books, video games, computer software, films and tv shows.
Online (CAWI) and face-to-face (CAPI) surveys were used.
